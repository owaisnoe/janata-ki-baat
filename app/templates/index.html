Nobody mails him back. Not because there's nothing to say — because envelopes are effort.

We are the effort.

FAQ — ASKED, ANSWERED
Are you affiliated with the Cockroach Janta Party?
No. We're independent people with a printer and stamps. No party — CJP or otherwise — gets your money, your data, or a say in this service.
Will anyone actually read my letter?
Physical correspondence to a ministry has to be received and logged. One letter is easy to ignore. A mailbag, less so. Volume is the point.
Where does my money go?
On the ledger, down to the paisa. Postage, paper, infrastructure — itemised in public. Look.
Is this even legal?
Writing a formal letter to a ministry is the most conventional form of civic petitioning there is. Letters stay formal and lawful — our content policy is strict about it: no threats, no abuse, no incitement.
What do you do with my details?
The minimum, briefly. Name and city go on the letter (that's the point). Email gets you your proof. Postal address is optional and purged 30 days after delivery. Nothing is sold, shared, or kept longer than needed — privacy page.
What if you don't post my letter?
Posted within 2 working days of payment confirmation, or the SLA has been blown and you get a full, automatic refund — refund policy.
