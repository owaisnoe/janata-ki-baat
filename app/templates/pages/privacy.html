PRIVACY — PLAIN ENGLISH, AS THE LAW INTENDS

We collect the minimum. We keep it briefly.

What we collect, and why

  • Name & city — printed on your letter. That's the point of the service.
  • Email — to send you payment confirmation and posting proof. Never printed, never shared.
  • Phone (optional) — only if you give it, only for order trouble.
  • Postal address (optional) — only if you want the Ministry to be able to reply to you. Printed inside the letter, purged from our systems 30 days after delivery.
  • Your personal paragraph — printed in the letter, screened against the content policy.
  • UTR / payment reference — to match your UPI payment.

What we never do

  • Sell, rent, or share your data with anyone — including any political party or movement.
  • Run advertising or tracking cookies. Analytics, if any, are Cloudflare's cookieless counts.
  • Keep reply addresses beyond 30 days post-delivery.

Proof photos

Envelope photos are visible in full only on your private status link (an unguessable code). Anything shown publicly has the sender line masked.

Your rights

Ask us what we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it (once your letter is posted, the postal record itself is out of our hands — that's rather the product). Write to {{ cfg.OPERATOR_CONTACT }}; we respond within 48 hours.

This page is our working notice under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023. Questions and complaints: {{ cfg.OPERATOR_CONTACT }}.
