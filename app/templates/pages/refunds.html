REFUNDS & THE SLA — THE PROMISE, IN WRITING

Posted in 2 working days, or your money back

The service promise

Every paid letter is posted within 2 working days of payment confirmation. You get an envelope photo and (Speed Post) a tracking number as proof, on your status page and by email.

Automatic refund

If your letter has not been posted within 5 days of payment confirmation, you get a full refund — automatically. You don't have to ask. Refunds go back on the same UPI rail the payment came from and appear on the public ledger.

Other refund cases

  • Content policy: if your personal paragraph breaks the rules and we decide not to post at all, full refund.
  • Our error: wrong letter, wrong details, lost order — full refund, no argument.
  • Change of mind: refundable any time before your letter enters a print run. After it's printed and posted, the letter is with India Post — there is no unsending a protest.

Unpaid orders

Orders with no confirmed payment expire after 24 hours. No money moved, nothing owed, the daily slot goes back in the pot.
