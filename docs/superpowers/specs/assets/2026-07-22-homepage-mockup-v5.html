THE PREMISE — FILED UNDER: DEMOCRATIC PAPERWORK

Every month, one man talks to 140 crore people. Nobody mails him back — not because there's nothing to say, but because envelopes are effort.

We are the effort.

You click. We print, stamp, queue, post, photograph, track. Your words land in Shastri Bhawan as a matter of record.

That's it. That's the service. The rest is postage. ✍
HOW IT WORKS

Three steps. Then it's on the record.

STEP / 01

Pick your words

Three formal letters, drafted to be received, logged and read. Add your name, your city, and a paragraph of your own.

STEP / 02

Pay the stamp

₹59 Speed Post or ₹19 ePost, itemised to the paisa on a public ledger. Postage, not politics.

STEP / 03

We post & prove it

Your envelope photographed at the counter, with an India Post tracking number. Impossible to un-receive.
